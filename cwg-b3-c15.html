To The Point
Not because it was impossible.
But because it was not inevitable.

Chapter 15 of Conversations with God — Book 3 makes one of the most radical claims in the entire trilogy: we are not only created by God — we are creating God. Every thought, word, and action becomes a statement about who we believe ourselves to be, and therefore a statement about what God is becoming through us. This project explores that claim from every angle — through the eyes of the skeptic and the believer, through the lens of freedom and the space between stimulus and response, and through the question of what makes a universe, a relationship, or a single choice valuable. The conclusion that emerged was not certainty, but participation: life as preparation meeting opportunity, and relationship as the place where the unexpected enters the world.
